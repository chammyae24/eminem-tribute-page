EMINEM

The Greatest Rapper of All Times

Here's a time line of Eminem's life:

Eminem is an American rapper, record producer and actor known as one of the most controversial and best-selling artists of the early 21st century.

  • 1972- born in St. Joseph, Missouri.
  • 1996- released his first independent rap album, Infinite.
  • 1997 released The Slim Shady EP, which was discovered by Dr. Dre, the legendary rapper and former producer of Eminem's favorite rap group N.W.A.
  • 1999- released the album named The Slim Shady LP. The album went multi-platinum, garnering Eminem two Grammy Awards and four MTV Video Music Awards.
  • 2000- released The Marshall Mathers LP, which was noted as the fastest-selling album in rap history.
  • 2001- reconnected with several of his friends from the Detroit underground rap scene to form the group D12, recording an album called Devil's Night featuring the popular single "Purple Pills."
  • 2002- delivered the Academy Award-winning song "Lose Yourself," from the semi-autobiographical drama 8 Mile andreleased a new solo album, The Eminem Show, another popular and critically acclaimed album highlighted by the tracks "Without Me," "Cleaning Out my Closet" and "Sing for the Moment."
  • 2004- released the album named Encore featured popular songs such as "Like Toy Soldiers" and "Mockingbird."
  • 2009- managed to kick his addictions to drugs and alcohol and returned to recording music. He released his first album of new music in five years, Relapse, featuring the singles "Crack a Bottle" and "Beautiful."
  • 2010- released the Grammy-winning album Recovery, a highly autobiographical attempt to come to terms with his struggles with addiction and experience with rehabilitation.
  • 2013- The Marshall Mathers LP 2. The announcement for the future Grammy-winning album, formally titled The Marshall Mathers LP 2, was made during the 2013 MTV Video Music Awards.
  • 2014- celebrated the 15th anniversary of his Shady Records label with a special two-CD set called ShadyXV. The collection features the label's most popular songs as well as some new material. The single "Guts Over Fear" quickly rose up the charts after its late October debut.
  • 2017- Revival. Its first two singles, "Walk on Water" and "River," featured collaborations with pop superstars Beyoncé and Ed Sheeran.
  • 2018- Kamikaze. The single "Not Alike" attacked rapper Machine Gun Kelly.
  • 2020- Music to Be Murdered By also Side B

Who is Eminem?

Rapper, actor and music producer Eminem is one of the best-selling musicians of the 21st century and one of the most influential rappers of all time.
